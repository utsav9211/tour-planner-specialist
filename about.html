India's trusted full-service tour planning company — turning travel dreams into unforgettable journeys since 2015.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 in New Delhi, Tour Planner Specialist started with a simple vision — to make professional, hassle-free travel accessible to every Indian. What began as a small team organising corporate offsites has grown into a nationwide tour planning company serving corporates, families, couples and educational institutions.

Today we serve 50,000+ happy travellers annually, partner with 500+ hotels and resorts across India and abroad, and are trusted by leading companies like TCS, Wipro, Infosys and 1,000+ schools for their travel needs.

We're proudly registered with the Indian Association of Tour Operators (IATO), certified by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, and ISO 9001:2015 compliant for quality management.

Our Mission

To democratise premium travel experiences — making them affordable, safe and memorable for every Indian, whether it's a first-time family holiday or a Fortune 500 corporate retreat.

Our Values

  • Trust First — Transparent pricing, no hidden costs
  • Safety Always — Insured trips, certified guides, emergency support
  • Indian Heart — Promoting responsible domestic tourism
  • Innovation — AI-powered recommendations, digital itineraries
